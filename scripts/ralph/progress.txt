- Files changed:

- **Migration approach:**
  - Uses `DISTINCT ON ("employeeId") ... ORDER BY "employeeId", "updatedAt" DESC` to select keeper (most recently updated active PDI)
  - Step 1: UPDATE PDIGoal.pdiId from non-keeper to keeper
  - Step 2: UPDATE PDIComment.pdiId from non-keeper to keeper
  - Step 3: UPDATE PDI.status = 'cancelled' for non-keeper active PDIs
  - Idempotent: after first run, no non-keeper active PDIs remain, so subsequent runs are no-ops
  - PDIEvidence is not touched directly — it's linked to PDIGoal, so reparenting goals automatically carries evidences
